Still Moment is your personal meditation app: import your own recordings, shape the timer to your liking — no subscription, no ads, no tracking. A gift, not a product.

YOUR LIBRARY, YOUR TASTE
You know which voices work for you. Still Moment helps you collect their meditations and have them always at hand.
• Import your own recordings — from your files or shared directly from the browser
• Organised by teacher — find what you're looking for quickly
• Listen with screen locked — put the phone down and let go
• Your files stay on your device

YOUR TIMER, YOUR PACE
Silence on your terms. No rigid format — shape the timer the way that feels right for you.
• 1 to 60 minutes — you decide the duration
• Gentle countdown to settle in
• Singing bowls for start, end, and intervals — choose what invites you in
• Background sound: complete silence or gentle forest sounds
• Volume adjusted to your feeling

CALM, CLEAR, WITHOUT DISTRACTION
Still Moment is intentionally simple. No points system, no statistics, no notifications pulling you out of stillness. Just space for your practice — designed in warm earth tones that don't agitate.

YOUR MEDITATION, YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE
Still Moment collects absolutely zero data. Everything stays on your device, where it belongs.
• No tracking, no analytics, no ads
• 100% offline — no internet required
• No account, no sign-up, no subscription
• Open source — verify our privacy claims yourself

FREE & OPEN SOURCE
Still Moment is built by a meditation practitioner, offered freely as a gift to the community. No premium features, no upsells, no hidden costs. The complete source code is available for anyone to review.
